Why Choose Water Ways?

Three pillars of excellence that set us apart in the sheet metal industry.

Premium Materials

We exclusively use high-grade copper and Rheinzink materials sourced for their superior durability, weather resistance, and natural beauty. Our commitment to quality materials ensures your investment stands the test of time.

  • 16-20 oz. copper sheet metal
  • Certified Rheinzink products
  • Marine-grade fasteners

Our Process

From initial consultation to final installation, we guide you through every step.

01

Consultation & Measurement

We begin with a detailed consultation to understand your project needs, aesthetic preferences, and functional requirements. Precise measurements are taken (remotely or on-site) to ensure perfect fit.

02

Custom Design & Quote

Our team develops a custom design tailored to your specifications, including material selection, finish options, and architectural details. We provide a comprehensive quote with transparent pricing and timeline.

03

Expert Fabrication

Your project is fabricated in our Arkansas facility using a combination of CNC precision and hand-crafting techniques. Each component is meticulously inspected before moving to the next stage.

04

Installation or Delivery

Depending on your location and project scope, we either install the work ourselves (regional projects) or carefully package and ship your custom metalwork with detailed installation instructions.
