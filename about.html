How We Work With You

Every Water Ways project follows a proven process designed to ensure your complete satisfaction.

01

Initial Consultation

Contact us by phone (479) 957-3794 or email (Ray@WaterwaysSheetMetal.com) with details about your project. Share photos of the area, measurements if available, and describe what you're trying to accomplish.

We'll discuss: Your goals and vision, functional requirements, aesthetic preferences, timeline expectations, and budget parameters.

02

Design & Engineering

Based on your consultation, we create detailed specifications for your custom metalwork. For complex projects, we develop designs showing exactly how your fabrication will look and function.

You'll receive: Precise measurements, material recommendations (copper, Rheinzink, stainless steel), design options, finish selections, and a detailed written quote.

03

Fabrication

Once you approve the design and quote, we begin fabrication. Your project moves through our shop in carefully controlled stages: CNC cutting for precision dimensions, hand-forming for artistic details, expert welding for watertight assembly, and finishing for quality assurance.

Progress updates: We'll send photos throughout fabrication so you can see your project taking shape.

04

Installation or Delivery

For regional projects, we offer professional installation. For projects beyond our service area, we carefully crate your custom metalwork in protective packaging and ship via common carrier to arrive safely and on schedule.

Quality assurance: Every project undergoes final inspection before leaving our shop.

Why We Specialize in Copper

Copper isn't just our material of choice—it's the gold standard for exterior metalwork, and for good reason.

Unmatched Longevity

Copper roofing and flashings routinely last 100+ years. Historic buildings across Europe prove copper's durability—many still have original copper installations from centuries ago. When properly installed, copper outlasts the structures it protects.

Natural Protection

Copper develops a protective patina over time—a natural weathering process that creates a beautiful green-blue finish while preventing corrosion. Unlike painted surfaces that require maintenance, copper's patina is self-healing and maintenance-free.

Superior Workability

Copper's malleability allows us to create complex curves, custom details, and precise fits that harder materials can't match. This workability means we can fabricate solutions for unique architectural challenges that other materials simply can't accommodate.

Sustainable & Valuable

Copper is 100% recyclable without loss of quality. At the end of a building's life (long after the copper), the material retains significant scrap value. It's an investment that holds value across generations.

Architectural Beauty

From bright metallic sheen to rich brown tones to classic green patina, copper offers evolving beauty that enhances any architectural style. It's the premium choice for homeowners and architects who understand that the best materials are worth the investment.
