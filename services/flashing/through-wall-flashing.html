How Through-Wall Flashing Protects Your Investment

Precision-engineered water management systems that work invisibly to protect your masonry structure

Water Interception at Critical Points

Through-wall flashing is installed within masonry wall cavities at strategic locations—above windows, doors, bay windows, and where walls meet other structures. The flashing intercepts water that penetrates the outer masonry layer before it reaches your building's interior structure.

What you get: Invisible protection that stops water at the source—keeping wall cavities, insulation, and framing completely dry.

Engineered Water Channeling System

Our custom copper flashing is fabricated with precise bends, slopes, and weep hole integration that channels intercepted water to the exterior. The system works with gravity and proper engineering to ensure water moves away from your structure—not into it.

What you get: Continuous water management that functions perfectly even during heavy rain events—no maintenance required.

Custom Fabrication for Perfect Fit

Every through-wall flashing system we create is custom-fabricated to your exact wall configuration—accounting for wall thickness, cavity dimensions, masonry patterns, and architectural details. This precision ensures the flashing integrates seamlessly and performs flawlessly.

What you get: Flashing that fits your structure exactly—no gaps, no compromises, no water infiltration.

Lifetime Copper Durability

We fabricate all through-wall flashing from premium copper that develops a protective patina over time. Unlike rubber or vinyl alternatives that degrade within 15-30 years, copper flashing lasts the entire lifetime of your building—100+ years of continuous protection.

What you get: Install it once and forget it—your flashing will outlast you, your children, and possibly your grandchildren.

Through-Wall Flashing Applications

Custom solutions for every masonry water management challenge

Bay Window Masonry Integration

Protect bay window structures with curved through-wall flashing systems that integrate seamlessly with masonry work. Our solutions channel water away from the complex angles and transitions where bay windows meet walls.

  • Custom curved flashing for bay window geometry
  • Integrated header and lintel protection
  • Proper weep hole placement for drainage
  • Coordination with masonry installation

Window & Door Headers

Prevent water infiltration above all window and door openings with precision-engineered header flashing. Our systems integrate with lintels and provide continuous protection across the entire opening width.

  • Custom lengths for any opening size
  • Proper slope for positive drainage
  • Integration with window/door systems
  • Multiple weep hole configurations

Parapet Walls & Balconies

Protect exposed wall tops and balcony transitions with comprehensive through-wall flashing systems. These high-exposure areas require robust water management to prevent accelerated deterioration.

  • Top-of-wall moisture barriers
  • Balcony perimeter protection
  • Coping stone integration
  • Multi-layer waterproofing systems

Chimney Wall Intersections

Where chimneys penetrate or meet masonry walls, through-wall flashing creates critical water barriers. Our systems ensure water cannot migrate from the chimney into adjacent wall cavities.

  • Chimney-to-wall transition protection
  • Integration with chimney flashing
  • Multi-sided protection systems
  • Coordination with chimney caps

Foundation & Grade-Level Protection

At grade level where walls meet foundations, through-wall flashing prevents ground moisture from wicking up into masonry. This critical barrier protects lower wall sections from constant moisture exposure.

  • Foundation transition barriers
  • Grade-level moisture protection
  • Coordination with waterproofing membranes
  • Weep hole systems for cavity drainage

Roof-to-Wall Transitions

Where rooflines intersect with masonry walls, through-wall flashing provides critical protection against water that runs down walls. Our systems integrate with roof flashing for complete weather protection.

  • Roof/wall intersection barriers
  • Integration with step flashing
  • Counter-flashing coordination
  • Multi-story building applications

Expert Installation Ensures Lifetime Performance

Through-wall flashing must be installed during masonry construction or carefully integrated during renovations. Our expertise ensures proper integration for flawless performance.

Coordination with Masonry Work

We work directly with your masonry contractor to ensure flashing is positioned correctly as walls are built. This coordination ensures proper placement, proper slope, and proper integration with weep holes—all critical for effective water management.

Precision Fabrication to Site Conditions

Each flashing piece is fabricated to match your exact wall configuration—accounting for cavity depth, masonry thickness, and architectural details. This precision eliminates gaps where water could bypass the flashing system.

Quality Control & Testing

Before masonry work proceeds, we verify flashing placement, slope, and drainage paths. This quality control step ensures the system will perform correctly before it's permanently enclosed within the wall.
