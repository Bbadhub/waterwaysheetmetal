Our Professional Installation Process

Every installation follows our proven methodology to ensure perfect fit, proper function, and lasting protection

Step 1: Precise Measurement & Assessment

We begin by carefully measuring your chimney's exact dimensions—accounting for flue size, crown condition, and structural considerations. This precision ensures your custom cap fits perfectly the first time, with no gaps or installation issues.

What you get: Detailed measurements, structural assessment, and custom fabrication specifications tailored to your specific chimney configuration.

Step 2: Custom Fabrication to Specifications

Your chimney cap is hand-crafted in our shop using premium 16oz copper. We combine CNC precision cutting with traditional metalworking techniques to create a cap engineered specifically for your chimney—ensuring proper ventilation, weather protection, and architectural harmony.

What you get: Custom-built cap that fits your exact specifications, crafted from materials proven to last 100+ years.

Step 3: Weather-Tight Installation

Our installation process ensures every seam, joint, and connection point is weather-tight. We secure the cap properly to withstand high winds while maintaining proper ventilation. All penetrations are sealed, and flashing is integrated to create a complete water-shedding system.

What you get: Professional installation that eliminates water intrusion while maintaining optimal draft and ventilation for your fireplace or heating system.

Step 4: Final Inspection & Performance Verification

Before we consider the job complete, we perform a comprehensive inspection—checking all seals, verifying proper ventilation, and ensuring the cap is securely fastened. We test draft performance and confirm the installation meets our exacting standards.

What you get: Peace of mind knowing your chimney cap is installed correctly and will perform flawlessly for decades to come.

Why Professional Installation Ensures Lifetime Performance

A custom copper chimney cap is a significant investment in your home's protection. Professional installation ensures you get the full value of that investment.

Precise Fit Prevents Future Problems

Ill-fitting caps allow water infiltration, create drafting issues, and can blow off in high winds. Our precision installation process ensures perfect fit from day one—eliminating these common problems before they start.

Proper Sealing Protects Your Investment

The connection between cap and chimney is critical. We use proven techniques to create weather-tight seals that prevent water penetration while allowing proper ventilation. This attention to detail protects both your cap and your chimney structure.

Engineering for Long-Term Performance

We don't just install caps—we engineer complete chimney protection systems. Every installation accounts for wind loads, thermal expansion, water shedding, and ventilation requirements. This comprehensive approach ensures your cap performs perfectly year after year.
