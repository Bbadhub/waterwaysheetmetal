Frequently Asked Questions

How long does copper last?

Copper chimney caps installed in the 1800s are still functioning today. Our 16 oz. copper will outlast your home's structure‚Äîeasily 100+ years with zero maintenance.

What about the green patina?

The verde patina is copper's natural protective layer‚Äîit prevents further corrosion and gives copper its distinctive look. It develops over 5-10 years depending on climate. You can also request pre-patina treatment.

How much does a custom cap cost?

Single flue caps start around $400-600. Multi-flue caps run $800-1,500. Full shrouds are $2,000-5,000+ depending on size and complexity. Every cap is custom-priced based on dimensions and design.

Do you make caps for prefab chimneys?

Yes! We specialize in custom chase covers for factory-built chimneys. These replace the galvanized pans that rust through in 5-10 years.

Can I get a cap without spark arrestor screen?

Yes, if your chimney isn't used for burning wood. For active fireplaces or wood stoves, we include stainless steel spark arrestor screen per building codes.

How do I measure my chimney?

Measure the outside dimensions of your chimney crown (the concrete top). For chase covers, measure the outer edges of the chase. We'll confirm measurements from photos before fabrication.
