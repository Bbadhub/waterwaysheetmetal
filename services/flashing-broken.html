Frequently Asked Questions

TWF is flashing installed INSIDE the wall assembly that intercepts water penetrating masonry and directs it outside through weep holes‚Äîbefore it can damage framing or insulation.

Is TWF required by code?

Yes‚ÄîIRC and IBC building codes require TWF systems for masonry veneer construction. Many builders skip it or use improper materials. We ensure code compliance.

How much does custom flashing cost?
